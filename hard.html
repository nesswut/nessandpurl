Row 1: edge st, p1, p2tog, k2 * yo, k2, p3, p2tog, k2;
repeat from * to last 5 sts, yo, k2, p2, edge st
Row 2: edge st, k1, sl1kw, k1, psso, p2, yo, * p2, k3, sl1kw, k1, psso, p2, yo;
repeat from * to last 5 sts, p2, k2, edge st

Repeat rows 1 through 2.

to last 4 sts, p3togm3, edge st
